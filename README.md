# JustEvolve
JustEvolve is a web application that helps users improve their posture and overall well-being by providing personalized yoga asanas recommendations and guidance. It uses neural networks and Posenet inputs to train a prediction model that can detect the correctness of the user's asanas. The app also features an AI chatbot based on natural language processing to help users describe their ailments and postural flaws and recommend asanas for repair.

This project is aimed at providing a user-friendly and personalized approach to yoga that will reduce the possibility of health problems and ailments that can occur when asanas are performed incorrectly. The app is accessible to all members of the group, and each individual can benefit from the service according to their specific needs.
